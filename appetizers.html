Classic Tomato Bruschetta

20 mins 6 ingredients 180 cal/serving

Crispy toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and a drizzle of aged balsamic. Perfect for summer entertaining.

Ingredients

1 baguette, sliced
4 ripe tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic glaze
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5-7 minutes until golden.
2
In a bowl, combine tomatoes, basil, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Let sit for 10 minutes.
3
Top each toast with the tomato mixture and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Garlic Herb Stuffed Mushrooms

35 mins 8 ingredients 120 cal/serving

Creamy, garlicky stuffed mushrooms with a crispy breadcrumb topping. An elegant appetizer that's always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

24 large cremini mushrooms
3 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup cream cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
2
Heat 1 tbsp oil in pan, sauté garlic and chopped mushroom stems for 5 minutes.
3
Mix with cream cheese, Parmesan, parsley, salt and pepper.
4
Stuff mushroom caps, top with breadcrumbs mixed with remaining oil.
5
Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls

45 mins 12 ingredients 150 cal/serving

Light and refreshing rice paper rolls filled with shrimp, herbs, and vegetables. Served with a tangy peanut dipping sauce.

Ingredients

12 rice paper wrappers
24 medium cooked shrimp
2 cups lettuce, shredded
1 cup bean sprouts
1 carrot, julienned
1 cucumber, julienned
1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
1/2 cup fresh cilantro
1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2 tbsp peanut butter
1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions

1
Prepare all vegetables and herbs. Peel and halve shrimp lengthwise.
2
Soak rice paper in warm water for 10-15 seconds until pliable.
3
Layer ingredients on bottom third of wrapper: lettuce, sprouts, carrots, cucumber, herbs, 4 shrimp halves.
4
Fold sides inward, then roll tightly from bottom. Repeat for all rolls.
5
For sauce: Mix hoisin, peanut butter, lime juice and 2 tbsp water. Top with peanuts.

Truffle Arancini with Fontina Center

1 hour 30 mins 14 ingredients 280 cal/serving

Crispy fried risotto balls infused with black truffle and stuffed with molten fontina cheese. A luxurious Italian appetizer.

Ingredients

2 cups Arborio rice
4 cups chicken stock
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 shallot, minced
2 tbsp black truffle paste
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
4 oz fontina cheese, cubed
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup breadcrumbs
1/2 cup flour
Vegetable oil for frying
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1 clove roasted garlic
1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

1
Cook risotto with stock, wine, and shallot. Stir in truffle paste and Parmesan. Chill 2 hours.
2
Form rice balls around fontina cubes. Chill 30 minutes.
3
Dredge in flour, egg, then breadcrumbs. Chill 15 minutes.
4
Fry at 375°F (190°C) until golden, about 4 minutes.
5
Mix mayo, roasted garlic, and lemon juice for dipping sauce.

Bacon-Wrapped Dates with Goat Cheese

30 mins 5 ingredients 210 cal/serving

Sweet Medjool dates stuffed with creamy goat cheese, wrapped in smoky bacon, and baked to perfection. Irresistible flavor combination.

Ingredients

24 Medjool dates, pitted
4 oz goat cheese
12 slices bacon, cut in half
2 tbsp honey
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheet with parchment.
2
Stuff each date with about 1 tsp goat cheese.
3
Wrap each date with half slice bacon, securing with toothpick.
4
Bake 20-25 minutes until bacon is crisp, turning once.
5
Drizzle with honey and sprinkle with red pepper flakes before serving.

Spinach and Feta Spanakopita Triangles

50 mins 9 ingredients 190 cal/serving

Flaky phyllo pastry filled with spinach, feta, and herbs. A classic Greek appetizer that's perfect for parties.

Ingredients

1 lb fresh spinach, chopped
1 cup feta cheese, crumbled
1/2 cup ricotta cheese
2 eggs, lightly beaten
3 green onions, chopped
2 tbsp fresh dill, chopped
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 cup melted butter
12 sheets phyllo dough

Instructions

1
Wilt spinach in pan, squeeze out excess water. Mix with cheeses, eggs, onions, dill and nutmeg.
2
Cut phyllo sheets into strips. Brush with butter, layer 3 strips.
3
Place spoonful of filling at end, fold into triangles.
4
Brush with butter, bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 mins until golden.

Fresh mozzarella, cherry tomatoes, and basil leaves skewered and drizzled with balsamic reduction. A no-cook appetizer that's always a hit.

8 oz fresh mozzarella balls (ciliegine)

Maryland-Style Crab Cakes

40 mins 10 ingredients 230 cal/serving

Jumbo lump crab meat lightly bound with minimal filler to let the sweet crab flavor shine. Served with remoulade sauce.

Ingredients

1 lb jumbo lump crab meat
1/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
1 egg, lightly beaten
2 tbsp mayonnaise
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tsp Old Bay seasoning
1 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

1
Gently pick through crab meat to remove any shells.
2
Mix all ingredients except crab. Gently fold in crab, keeping lumps intact.
3
Form into 8 patties. Chill 30 minutes.
4
Heat butter and oil in skillet. Cook cakes 4-5 mins per side until golden.

Mediterranean Hummus Platter

25 mins 12 ingredients 180 cal/serving

Creamy homemade hummus surrounded by fresh vegetables, olives, and warm pita. A healthy, colorful starter for any gathering.

Ingredients

2 cans chickpeas, drained
1/4 cup tahini
3 tbsp lemon juice
2 cloves garlic
1/2 tsp cumin
1/4 cup olive oil
2 tbsp water
1 cucumber, sliced
1 cup cherry tomatoes
1/2 cup Kalamata olives
4 pita breads, warmed
1/4 cup fresh parsley

Instructions

1
Blend chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, cumin until smooth.
2
With motor running, drizzle in olive oil and water to desired consistency.
3
Spread hummus on large platter, creating wells with back of spoon.
4
Drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with paprika.
5
Arrange vegetables, olives, and pita around hummus. Garnish with parsley.
