Herb-Crusted Ribeye Steak

30 mins 8 ingredients 450 cal/serving

Perfectly cooked ribeye with a fragrant herb crust, served with red wine reduction and roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

2 ribeye steaks (1.5" thick)
2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
2 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup red wine
2 tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
2
Sear steaks in hot pan for 3 minutes per side. Remove from pan.
3
Mix herbs, garlic, mustard and breadcrumbs. Press onto steaks.
4
Roast in oven for 6-8 minutes for medium-rare.
5
Deglaze pan with red wine, reduce by half, whisk in butter for sauce.

Miso Glazed Salmon

25 mins 7 ingredients 320 cal/serving

Silky salmon fillets with sweet-savory miso glaze, served with sesame roasted broccoli and jasmine rice.

Ingredients

4 salmon fillets (6oz each)
3 tbsp white miso paste
2 tbsp mirin
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tsp grated ginger
1 tbsp sesame seeds
2 heads broccoli, cut into florets

Instructions

1
Whisk miso, mirin, honey, vinegar and ginger to make glaze.
2
Pat salmon dry, brush with half the glaze. Let marinate 15 minutes.
3
Toss broccoli with oil, salt and sesame seeds. Roast at 425°F (220°C) for 15 minutes.
4
Broil salmon 6" from heat for 6-8 minutes until caramelized.
5
Serve with remaining glaze drizzled over, alongside broccoli.

Creamy Mushroom Fettuccine

35 mins 10 ingredients 580 cal/serving

Rich and creamy pasta with wild mushrooms, garlic, white wine, and Parmesan. A restaurant-quality dish made at home.

Ingredients

12 oz fettuccine pasta
3 tbsp olive oil
1 lb mixed wild mushrooms
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 shallot, finely chopped
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Cook pasta al dente according to package directions.
2
Heat oil in large skillet. Sauté mushrooms until golden (8-10 mins).
3
Add garlic and shallot, cook 2 minutes until fragrant.
4
Deglaze with wine, simmer until reduced by half.
5
Stir in cream and Parmesan until smooth. Toss with pasta.
6
Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan before serving.

Coconut Chicken Curry

45 mins 12 ingredients 420 cal/serving

Fragrant curry with tender chicken, creamy coconut milk, and aromatic spices. Served with basmati rice and naan bread.

Ingredients

1.5 lbs chicken thighs, boneless
1 onion, finely chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp ginger, grated
2 tbsp curry powder
1 tsp turmeric
1 can (14oz) coconut milk
1 cup chicken stock
2 tomatoes, diced
1 red bell pepper, sliced
2 tbsp vegetable oil
Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1
Heat oil in large pot. Brown chicken on both sides, then remove.
2
Sauté onion until soft, add garlic, ginger, and spices. Cook 1 minute.
3
Add tomatoes and bell pepper, cook until tomatoes break down.
4
Return chicken to pot, add coconut milk and stock. Simmer 25 minutes.
5
Season with salt, garnish with cilantro. Serve with rice and naan.

Classic Beef Bourguignon

3 hours 14 ingredients 520 cal/serving

Tender beef slow-cooked in red wine with mushrooms, carrots, and pearl onions. A French classic that's perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

3 lbs beef chuck, cubed
6 oz bacon, diced
2 carrots, sliced
1 onion, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp tomato paste
2 cups red wine
2 cups beef stock
1 lb mushrooms, quartered
1 cup pearl onions, peeled
2 tbsp flour
3 sprigs fresh thyme
2 bay leaves
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 325°F (165°C). Cook bacon in Dutch oven until crisp, then remove.
2
Sear beef in batches in bacon fat until browned. Remove and set aside.
3
Sauté carrots and onion until softened. Add garlic and tomato paste, cook 1 minute.
4
Sprinkle flour over vegetables, stir to combine. Add wine and stock, bring to simmer.
5
Return beef and bacon to pot. Add herbs. Cover and braise in oven for 2.5 hours.
6
Add mushrooms and pearl onions, cook 30 minutes more. Season and serve.

Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

1 hour 12 ingredients 280 cal/serving

Colorful bell peppers stuffed with protein-packed quinoa, black beans, corn, and cheese. A satisfying meatless main.

Ingredients

4 large bell peppers
1 cup quinoa, rinsed
1 can black beans, drained
1 cup corn kernels
1 small onion, diced
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1 cup shredded cheese
1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
1 lime, juiced
2 cups vegetable broth

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Cut tops off peppers, remove seeds.
2
Sauté onion and garlic until soft. Add spices, cook 1 minute.
3
Add quinoa, broth, beans and corn. Bring to boil, then simmer 15 minutes.
4
Stir in half the cheese, lime juice and cilantro. Stuff peppers.
5
Top with remaining cheese. Bake 25-30 minutes until peppers are tender.

Honey Garlic Pork Tenderloin

40 mins 9 ingredients 320 cal/serving

Juicy pork tenderloin with a sticky honey garlic glaze, roasted with carrots and potatoes for a complete meal.

Ingredients

1.5 lbs pork tenderloin
3 tbsp honey
4 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp Dijon mustard
1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 lb baby potatoes, halved
3 carrots, sliced
2 tbsp olive oil

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Whisk honey, garlic, soy sauce, mustard and vinegar.
2
Toss potatoes and carrots with oil, salt and pepper. Roast for 15 minutes.
3
Sear pork in hot pan on all sides. Brush with half the glaze.
4
Add pork to roasting pan with vegetables. Roast 15-20 minutes until 145°F internal temp.
5
Let rest 5 minutes, slice and serve with remaining glaze.

Eggplant Parmesan

1 hour 15 mins 12 ingredients 380 cal/serving

Layers of crispy breaded eggplant, rich tomato sauce, and melted mozzarella. A vegetarian classic that satisfies everyone.

Ingredients

2 large eggplants, sliced
1 cup flour
3 eggs, beaten
2 cups breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
2 cups marinara sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella
1/2 cup fresh basil, chopped
1 tsp dried oregano
1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
Olive oil for frying
Salt to taste

Instructions

1
Salt eggplant slices, let sit 30 minutes to draw out moisture. Pat dry.
2
Set up breading station: flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs mixed with Parmesan.
3
Dredge eggplant in flour, dip in egg, then coat with breadcrumbs.
4
Fry in batches until golden on both sides. Drain on paper towels.
5
Layer sauce, eggplant, mozzarella and herbs in baking dish. Repeat layers.
6
Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 minutes until bubbly and golden.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi

25 mins 10 ingredients 350 cal/serving

Plump shrimp sautéed in garlic butter with white wine and lemon, served over linguine. An elegant yet quick dinner.

Ingredients

1 lb large shrimp, peeled
8 oz linguine
4 tbsp butter
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
1/2 cup white wine
1 lemon, zested and juiced
1/4 cup parsley, chopped
2 tbsp olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Cook pasta according to package directions. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water.
2
Heat oil and 2 tbsp butter in large skillet. Add shrimp, cook 2 minutes per side. Remove.
3
Add remaining butter, garlic and red pepper flakes. Cook 30 seconds until fragrant.
4
Deglaze with wine, simmer 2 minutes. Stir in lemon juice and zest.
5
Return shrimp to pan with pasta, tossing to coat. Add pasta water as needed.
6
Garnish with parsley and serve immediately.
