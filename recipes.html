Classic Tomato Bruschetta

20 mins 6 ingredients 180 cal/serving

Crispy toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, garlic, and a drizzle of aged balsamic. Perfect for summer entertaining.

Ingredients

1 baguette, sliced
4 ripe tomatoes, diced
1/4 cup fresh basil, chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 tbsp balsamic glaze
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Arrange baguette slices on a baking sheet and toast for 5-7 minutes until golden.
2
In a bowl, combine tomatoes, basil, garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Let sit for 10 minutes.
3
Top each toast with the tomato mixture and drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Garlic Herb Stuffed Mushrooms

35 mins 8 ingredients 120 cal/serving

Creamy, garlicky stuffed mushrooms with a crispy breadcrumb topping. An elegant appetizer that's always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

24 large cremini mushrooms
3 tbsp olive oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 cup cream cheese
1/4 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
1/4 cup breadcrumbs
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Clean mushrooms and remove stems.
2
Heat 1 tbsp oil in pan, sauté garlic and chopped mushroom stems for 5 minutes.
3
Mix with cream cheese, Parmesan, parsley, salt and pepper.
4
Stuff mushroom caps, top with breadcrumbs mixed with remaining oil.
5
Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden and bubbly.

Vietnamese Fresh Spring Rolls

45 mins 12 ingredients 150 cal/serving

Light and refreshing rice paper rolls filled with shrimp, herbs, and vegetables. Served with a tangy peanut dipping sauce.

Ingredients

12 rice paper wrappers
24 medium cooked shrimp
2 cups lettuce, shredded
1 cup bean sprouts
1 carrot, julienned
1 cucumber, julienned
1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
1/2 cup fresh cilantro
1/4 cup roasted peanuts, chopped
1/4 cup hoisin sauce
2 tbsp peanut butter
1 tbsp lime juice

Instructions

1
Prepare all vegetables and herbs. Peel and halve shrimp lengthwise.
2
Soak rice paper in warm water for 10-15 seconds until pliable.
3
Layer ingredients on bottom third of wrapper: lettuce, sprouts, carrots, cucumber, herbs, 4 shrimp halves.
4
Fold sides inward, then roll tightly from bottom. Repeat for all rolls.
5
For sauce: Mix hoisin, peanut butter, lime juice and 2 tbsp water. Top with peanuts.

Herb-Crusted Ribeye Steak

30 mins 8 ingredients 450 cal/serving

Perfectly cooked ribeye with a fragrant herb crust, served with red wine reduction and roasted vegetables.

Ingredients

2 ribeye steaks (1.5" thick)
2 tbsp fresh rosemary, chopped
2 tbsp fresh thyme, chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
2 tbsp Dijon mustard
1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
1/2 cup red wine
2 tbsp butter
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Season steaks generously with salt and pepper.
2
Sear steaks in hot pan for 3 minutes per side. Remove from pan.
3
Mix herbs, garlic, mustard and breadcrumbs. Press onto steaks.
4
Roast in oven for 6-8 minutes for medium-rare.
5
Deglaze pan with red wine, reduce by half, whisk in butter for sauce.

Miso Glazed Salmon

25 mins 7 ingredients 320 cal/serving

Silky salmon fillets with sweet-savory miso glaze, served with sesame roasted broccoli and jasmine rice.

Ingredients

4 salmon fillets (6oz each)
3 tbsp white miso paste
2 tbsp mirin
1 tbsp honey
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tsp grated ginger
1 tbsp sesame seeds
2 heads broccoli, cut into florets

Instructions

1
Whisk miso, mirin, honey, vinegar and ginger to make glaze.
2
Pat salmon dry, brush with half the glaze. Let marinate 15 minutes.
3
Toss broccoli with oil, salt and sesame seeds. Roast at 425°F (220°C) for 15 minutes.
4
Broil salmon 6" from heat for 6-8 minutes until caramelized.
5
Serve with remaining glaze drizzled over, alongside broccoli.

Creamy Mushroom Fettuccine

35 mins 10 ingredients 580 cal/serving

Rich and creamy pasta with wild mushrooms, garlic, white wine, and Parmesan. A restaurant-quality dish made at home.

Ingredients

12 oz fettuccine pasta
3 tbsp olive oil
1 lb mixed wild mushrooms
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 shallot, finely chopped
1/2 cup dry white wine
1 cup heavy cream
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Cook pasta al dente according to package directions.
2
Heat oil in large skillet. Sauté mushrooms until golden (8-10 mins).
3
Add garlic and shallot, cook 2 minutes until fragrant.
4
Deglaze with wine, simmer until reduced by half.
5
Stir in cream and Parmesan until smooth. Toss with pasta.
6
Garnish with parsley and extra Parmesan before serving.

Molten Chocolate Lava Cakes

30 mins 7 ingredients 420 cal/serving

Individual chocolate cakes with a gooey, molten center. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream for the ultimate dessert experience.

Ingredients

6 oz high-quality dark chocolate
1/2 cup unsalted butter
2 whole eggs
2 egg yolks
1/4 cup granulated sugar
2 tbsp all-purpose flour
Pinch of salt
Powdered sugar for dusting

Instructions

1
Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Butter and flour 4 ramekins.
2
Melt chocolate and butter together in double boiler, then cool slightly.
3
Whisk eggs, yolks and sugar until pale and thick (about 5 mins).
4
Fold chocolate mixture into eggs, then fold in flour and salt.
5
Divide batter among ramekins. Bake 12-14 minutes until edges are set but center is soft.
6
Let sit 1 minute, then invert onto plates. Dust with powdered sugar.

Berry Swirl Cheesecake

6 hours 10 ingredients 380 cal/serving

Creamy no-bake cheesecake with a graham cracker crust and vibrant mixed berry swirl. Refreshing and perfect for summer.

Ingredients

2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup melted butter
2 tbsp sugar
16 oz cream cheese, softened
1 cup powdered sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup heavy cream, whipped
1 cup mixed berries
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions

1
Mix crumbs, melted butter and sugar. Press into 9" springform pan. Chill 30 mins.
2
Beat cream cheese until smooth. Add powdered sugar and vanilla.
3
Fold in whipped cream. Spread over crust.
4
Purée berries with lemon juice. Strain, then cook with cornstarch until thickened.
5
Drizzle berry sauce over cheesecake and swirl with knife.
6
Chill at least 4 hours, preferably overnight.

Authentic Italian Tiramisu

45 mins 9 ingredients 350 cal/serving

Layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and mascarpone cream, dusted with cocoa powder. The ultimate no-bake Italian dessert.

Ingredients

6 egg yolks
3/4 cup granulated sugar
16 oz mascarpone cheese
1 1/2 cups heavy cream
2 cups strong espresso, cooled
3 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
24 ladyfinger cookies
1/4 cup cocoa powder
Dark chocolate shavings

Instructions

1
Whisk yolks and sugar over double boiler until pale and thick (about 10 mins).
2
Remove from heat, beat in mascarpone until smooth.
3
Whip cream to stiff peaks, then fold into mascarpone mixture.
4
Mix espresso with liqueur. Quickly dip ladyfingers (1-2 seconds per side).
5
Layer dipped ladyfingers, then cream mixture in 9x13" dish. Repeat layers.
6
Chill 6 hours or overnight. Dust with cocoa and chocolate shavings before serving.

Rainbow Buddha Bowl

35 mins 12 ingredients 420 cal/serving

Nutrient-packed bowl with quinoa, roasted vegetables, avocado, and tahini dressing. Completely customizable to your taste.

Ingredients

1 cup quinoa, rinsed
1 sweet potato, cubed
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 zucchini, sliced
1 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 avocado, sliced
1 cup chickpeas, drained
2 cups baby spinach
1/4 cup tahini
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp maple syrup
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Cook quinoa according to package directions.
2
Toss sweet potato, bell pepper and zucchini with oil, paprika, salt and pepper. Roast at 400°F (200°C) for 25 minutes.
3
Whisk tahini, lemon juice, maple syrup and 3 tbsp water to make dressing.
4
Assemble bowls: quinoa base, roasted veggies, chickpeas, avocado and spinach.
5
Drizzle with tahini dressing before serving.

Eggplant Parmesan

1 hour 11 ingredients 380 cal/serving

Crispy breaded eggplant layered with marinara and melted cheeses. A vegetarian alternative to chicken parm that's just as satisfying.

Ingredients

2 large eggplants
1 tsp salt
2 eggs, beaten
1 cup breadcrumbs
1/2 cup grated Parmesan
1 tsp Italian seasoning
3 cups marinara sauce
2 cups shredded mozzarella
1/2 cup fresh basil
Olive oil for frying
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Slice eggplant into 1/2" rounds. Sprinkle with salt and let sit 30 mins to draw out moisture.
2
Pat dry. Dip in egg, then breadcrumb-Parmesan-herb mixture.
3
Fry in oil until golden (3-4 mins per side). Drain on paper towels.
4
In baking dish, layer sauce, eggplant, mozzarella. Repeat layers.
5
Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25-30 mins until bubbly and golden.
6
Garnish with fresh basil before serving.

Thai Green Vegetable Curry

40 mins 14 ingredients 320 cal/serving

Fragrant coconut curry packed with seasonal vegetables and Thai flavors. Serve with jasmine rice for a complete meal.

Ingredients

2 tbsp green curry paste
1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
1 cup vegetable broth
1 tbsp brown sugar
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp lime juice
1 red bell pepper, sliced
1 cup eggplant, cubed
1 cup zucchini, sliced
1 cup mushrooms, quartered
1 cup baby corn
1/2 cup bamboo shoots
1/2 cup Thai basil leaves
Jasmine rice for serving

Instructions

1
Heat 1/4 cup coconut cream from can in large pot. Fry curry paste 1-2 mins until fragrant.
2
Add remaining coconut milk, broth, sugar, soy sauce and lime juice. Bring to simmer.
3
Add vegetables (starting with hardest first). Simmer 15-20 mins until tender.
4
Stir in basil just before serving. Adjust seasoning if needed.
5
Serve hot over jasmine rice.
