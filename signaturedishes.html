Black Truffle & Wild Mushroom Arancini

1 tsp truffle oil
Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

1
Cook Arborio rice with white wine and chicken stock until creamy. Stir in Parmesan and truffle paste. Spread on tray to cool.

3
Take 2 tbsp risotto, make indentation, add cheese cube, and form into ball. Repeat.
4

5
Fry at 350Â°F (175Â°C) until golden (3-4 mins). Drain on paper towels.
6
Mix mayo with truffle oil for dipping sauce. Serve arancini warm.

Yuzu-Marinated Scallop Ceviche

30 mins 9 ingredients 5/5

Dayboat scallops cured in a vibrant yuzu-leche de tigre with finger lime caviar, compressed watermelon, and edible flowers. A perfect balance of sweet, tart, and umami flavors.

View Recipe

Ingredients

12 large fresh scallops
1/4 cup yuzu juice
2 tbsp lime juice
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp honey
1/2 cup seedless watermelon, diced
1 tbsp finger lime pearls
Edible flowers for garnish
Micro cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1
Slice scallops horizontally into 3 thin rounds each. Chill.
2
Whisk yuzu juice, lime juice, soy sauce, and honey to make marinade.
3
Place watermelon cubes under vacuum seal (or in ziplock with air removed) to compress for 10 mins.
4
Arrange scallop slices on plates. Spoon marinade over.
5
Add compressed watermelon, finger lime pearls, and garnish with flowers and micro cilantro.
6
Serve immediately with crisp white wine.

Coffee-Crusted Duck Breast

1 hour 14 ingredients 4.8/5

Perfectly cooked duck breast with an espresso-spice crust, served with cherry-port reduction, duck fat potatoes, and caramelized endive. The coffee enhances the natural richness of the duck.

View Recipe

Ingredients

2 duck breasts
1 tbsp finely ground espresso
1 tsp smoked paprika
1 tsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 cup port wine
1/2 cup cherries, pitted
1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
2 cups baby potatoes, halved
2 tbsp duck fat
2 endives, halved
1 tbsp honey
2 sprigs thyme
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

1
Score duck skin in crosshatch pattern. Season with salt.
2
Mix espresso, paprika, sugar, and cinnamon. Rub on duck flesh side.
3
Cook duck skin-side down in cold pan over medium heat until crispy (8-10 mins). Flip and cook 3-4 mins. Rest.
4
Simmer port, cherries, and vinegar until syrupy (10 mins).
5
Toss potatoes in duck fat, roast at 400Â°F (200Â°C) until golden (25 mins).
6
Sear endive cut-side down with honey until caramelized.
7
Slice duck, serve with sauce, potatoes, and endive.

Miso-Glazed Halibut

50 mins 11 ingredients 4.9/5

Wild-caught halibut with a caramelized white miso glaze, served with forbidden black rice, bok choy, and a dashi broth foam. The umami flavors create an unforgettable dining experience.

View Recipe

Ingredients

2 halibut fillets (6oz each)
3 tbsp white miso paste
1 tbsp mirin
1 tbsp honey
1 tsp grated ginger
1 cup forbidden black rice
2 cups dashi stock
2 baby bok choy, halved
1 tbsp soy sauce
1/4 cup heavy cream
1 tsp lecithin (for foam)

Instructions

1
Cook black rice according to package instructions using dashi instead of water.
2
Mix miso, mirin, honey, and ginger. Brush on halibut and marinate 30 mins.
3
Broil halibut 6" from heat for 6-8 mins untilglaze caramelizes.
4
Blanch bok choy in salted water, finish with soy sauce.
5
Heat remaining dashi with cream, blend with lecithin to create foam.
6
Plate rice, top with halibut, bok choy, and spoon foam around.

Chocolate Sphere with Liquid Caramel

2 hours 16 ingredients 5/5

A dramatic dark chocolate sphere filled with salted caramel mousse, chocolate crumble, and liquid caramel center. Served with warm caramel sauce poured tableside to melt the sphere.

View Recipe

Ingredients

8 oz dark chocolate (70%)
1/2 cup heavy cream
1/4 cup sugar
2 tbsp water
1/4 cup butter
1 tsp sea salt
1 cup chocolate cookie crumbs
1/2 cup caramel sauce
1 tsp gelatin
1/2 cup mascarpone
1/2 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup whipped cream
Gold leaf for garnish
Vanilla ice cream for serving
Edible flowers for garnish

Instructions

1
Melt chocolate and brush inside sphere molds. Chill until set (3 coats).
2
Make caramel: Cook sugar and water to amber, whisk in cream and butter, add salt.
3
Bloom gelatin in 1 tbsp water. Mix with 1/4 cup caramel, mascarpone, powdered sugar, and vanilla. Fold in whipped cream.
4
Pipe mousse into spheres, add cookie crumbs, then pour in remaining caramel. Freeze.
5
Unmold spheres, garnish with gold leaf and flowers.
6
Serve with warm caramel sauce and ice cream.
